Service Description

musicMixer is a web application that lets users create AI-powered audio remixes. Users upload two songs, describe the remix they want, and musicMixer uses AI to separate stems, match tempo and key, and generate a remix for playback.

SMS Notifications

musicMixer offers optional SMS notifications via Twilio to let you know when your remix is ready. By entering your phone number on the progress screen, you consent to receive up to two (2) text messages per remix:

  1. A confirmation that your number was received
  2. A notification when your remix is ready, with a link to listen

You will receive a maximum of 2 messages per remix request. No recurring, promotional, or marketing messages are sent.

Opt-Out

Text STOP to any message to stop receiving SMS notifications. You will receive a single confirmation message. You can also opt out by simply not entering your phone number.

Ephemeral Content

All remixes are temporary. Remixes are available for playback for up to 3 hours or until a new remix is created, then they are permanently deleted.
