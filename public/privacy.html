Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

What We Collect

musicMixer collects the minimum data needed to provide the service:

  • Audio files: The two songs you upload for remixing. These are processed temporarily and deleted after your remix expires (3 hours max).
  • Remix prompt: The text description you provide for your remix. This is used solely to generate your remix and is not stored permanently.
  • Phone number (optional): If you opt in to SMS notifications, your phone number is collected to send up to 2 text messages. Your phone number is deleted immediately after the notification is delivered.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to provide the musicMixer service:

  • Audio files are processed by AI to separate stems and generate your remix
  • Phone numbers are used exclusively to send SMS notifications via Twilio
  • We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, analytics, or user profiling

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate musicMixer:

  • Twilio: Sends and receives SMS messages. Twilio processes your phone number and message content according to Twilio's Privacy Policy.
  • AI processing services: Audio files and remix prompts are sent to AI services for stem separation and remix generation. No personal information is included in these requests.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, solely to provide the musicMixer service.

Data Retention

  • Audio files and remixes: Deleted automatically after 3 hours or when a new remix is created
  • Phone numbers: Deleted immediately after the SMS notification is delivered
  • No persistent user data: musicMixer has no user accounts and stores no long-term personal data

Your Rights

You can stop SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message. Since musicMixer stores no persistent personal data, there is nothing to request deletion of — your data is already gone.

Changes to This Policy
