SMS Notifications

musicMixer offers optional SMS notifications to let you know when your audio remix is ready.

How it works

While your remix is processing, you can tap "Text me when it's ready" and enter your phone number. This is entirely optional — you can always wait on the page instead.

Consent

By entering your phone number, you agree to receive up to two (2) text messages from musicMixer:

  1. A confirmation that we received your number
  2. A notification when your remix is ready, with a link to listen

No marketing messages will ever be sent. Your phone number is deleted immediately after the notification is delivered.

Message frequency

Maximum of 2 messages per remix. No recurring messages.

Opt-out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out. You can also simply close the dialog without entering your number.

Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply.
